# ClipTalk ✂️

**Just say it. It's edited.**

ClipTalk is an AI video-editing agent. Instead of dragging clips on a 
timeline, you simply describe what you want in natural language — 
"merge all his speaking clips", "cut out Mr. Wang's scenes", "make a 
60s highlight" — and the agent understands your footage, calls the 
right tools, and delivers the edited clips.

- 🗣️ **Conversational editing** — describe the task, get the result
- 🎯 **Content-aware** — identifies speakers, faces, and topics in your footage
- ⚡ **Agent-powered** — automatically plans and executes multi-step edits
